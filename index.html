HILANCO

Hungarian Intelligent Language Applications Consortium

HILANCO is a Natural Language Processing Consortium in Hungary, between Eötvös Loránd Research Network, Research Institute for Linguistics and University of Pécs, Applied Data Science and Artifical Intelligence Centre. Main goal of the Consortium is to research and populate Artifical Intelligence, especially Natural Language Processing technologies. The work of the Consortium exists on Hungarian and International levels, however there's a clear focus on Hungarian language processing and intelligent language applications.

Demos

Anonimizer
Choosable Hungarian topic anonimizer, based on Hungarian BERT-large model (HilBERT). Supported by Microsoft Hungary.

QA search engine
Hungarian Question Answer search engine, based on Transformer (HilBERT) technologies.

KRESZ search engine
Hungarian KRESZ search engine, based on Transformer (HilBERT) technologies.

Named Entity Recognition
Hungarian Named Entity Recognition, based on szegedNER corpus and Hungarian BERT-large model (HilBERT).

Extractive summarizer
Hungarian extractive summarizer demo, based on Transformer (HilBERT) technologies.

Named Entity Recognition API
Hungarian Named Entity Recognition API, based on szegedNER corpus and Hungarian BERT-large model (HilBERT).

Abstractive summarizer
Hungarian abstractive summarizer demo from NYTI. Based on various transformer technologies.

Document classifier / tagger
Hungarian news article classification or tag recommendation tool.
